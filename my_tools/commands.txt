adb shell rm /data/misc/camera/*.raw

pull all pics out:
adb shell ls /data/misc/camera/*.raw | tr "\n\r" " " | xargs -n1 adb pull

pull a specific one out:
adb pull /dir/file_name

find out where the raw files are:
find -name *.raw
